Recently, a post of this image, titled Recent mudslide on Hwy 99 started at non-deactivated logging road from a 1991 cutblock garnered over 1100 upvotes on the r/vancouver subreddit.

On their page is photos from all across British Columbia. I just think its neat to be able to see the direct efforts of an entire arm of the government.
